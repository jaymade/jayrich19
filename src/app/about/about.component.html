About Jay Rich

I was raised in the Carolinas and still call em’ home. However, I do like to travel and share my photos.

Drawing, painting, and and photography have been and interest throughout my life. So I enrolled at the School of Communication Art in the late 90’s and naturally spent a of majority my professional career designing. I created anything from brochures, photo & video production, to a massive product catalog development.

With the founding of digital age, my projects expanded into creating web sites, content management and app development. As I delved more into server / client model, I grew more interested in how computers work and communicated with each other. I enrolled in school again to learn network management and cyber security to better prepare for data.
